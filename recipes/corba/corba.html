Potato Soup

Description

A potato soup recipe that will make you addicted to my grandma's special recipe.

Ingredients.

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 6 cups mashed cooked potatoes
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • Â½ cup milk

Steps

  1. In a medium soup pot melt butter over low heat, and saute onions until tender.
  2. slowly add the chicken broth.
    • Stirring, add milk (use more or less to achieve desired creaminess).
  3. Cook until heated through and season with salt and pepper to taste.
